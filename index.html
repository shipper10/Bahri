CGPA Formula
CGPA = Σ (Year GPA × Weight)

Year GPA = Total Quality Points / Total Credit Hours
(across both semesters combined)

Sem GPA = Σ(Grade Points × Credit Hours) / Σ(Credit Hours)

Example:
Year 5 GPA = 3.60, Weight = 20%
Contribution = 3.60 × 0.20 = 0.720
